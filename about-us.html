About Us

At Sport Air, we believe a great sporting event should be more than just a ticket in your hand — it should be a complete travel experience. We specialise in crafting tailored packages for the world’s biggest sports, including Golf, Formula 1, Tennis, Soccer, and Football.

From securing the best seats in the stadium to arranging premium hotels, transfers, and exclusive hospitality options, our team handles the details so you can focus on the excitement of the game.

Our Story

Sport Air was founded with one goal: to connect fans with unforgettable sporting experiences across the globe. We’ve been in the sports travel industry for years, building partnerships with trusted ticketing providers, top hotels, and local operators to deliver packages that go beyond expectations.

Whether it’s walking into Augusta National for The Masters, hearing the engines roar at the Monaco Grand Prix, or celebrating under the lights at a World Cup final, we understand what these moments mean to fans — because we’re fans too.
