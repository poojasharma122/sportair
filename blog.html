Best Family Hotels in London for a Premier League Weekend

A football trip to London can be just as enjoyable for families as it is for die-hard fans — if you choose the right place to stay. These hotels offer comfort, convenience, and amenities for all ages.

Park Plaza Westminster Bridge

📍 Central London

Spacious family rooms, an indoor swimming pool, and walking access to major landmarks make this an ideal base. Its central location also offers quick train links to stadiums like Stamford Bridge and the Emirates.

Premier Inn London City (Aldgate)

📍 East London

Budget-friendly without sacrificing comfort, this hotel is perfect for families who want a practical base close to Tower Bridge and easy access to Arsenal’s Emirates Stadium or West Ham’s London Stadium.

The Athenaeum Hotel & Residences

📍 Mayfair

For a luxury family experience, The Athenaeum offers apartments with kitchens, a kids’ concierge, and Green Park right outside. Great for combining football with sightseeing in London’s most elegant neighbourhood.

With Sport Air, your London Premier League package can include match tickets, family-friendly hotels, and city tours that keep everyone entertained — even on non-match days.

Top Party Hotels in Singapore for the Grand Prix

Singapore’s night race is one of Formula 1’s most exciting weekends — and staying at a hotel that keeps the energy going can make your trip even better.

Marina Bay Sands

📍 Marina Bay

An icon of the city skyline, Marina Bay Sands offers an infinity pool with panoramic views and multiple bars and lounges. Many suites have track views, and you can walk to parts of the circuit.

The Fullerton Bay Hotel

📍 Marina Bay Waterfront

Known for its rooftop Lantern bar, this hotel blends elegance with a lively atmosphere. A perfect choice for those wanting pre- and post-race cocktails with a view.

SO/ Singapore

📍 Downtown Core

Trendy interiors, a rooftop pool, and a buzzing in-house bar make this boutique hotel a hotspot for F1 after-parties.

Sport Air Singapore Grand Prix packages can add VIP party passes, rooftop bar reservations, and access to exclusive race weekend events so your stay is as thrilling as the race itself.

Luxury Adults-Only Resorts in the Maldives for Post-Event Relaxation

After the buzz of a major sporting event, nothing beats unwinding in a quiet, adults-only setting — and the Maldives is the perfect place to do it.

Hurawalhi Island Resort

📍 Lhaviyani Atoll

This five-star escape is known for its overwater villas and the world’s largest all-glass underwater restaurant. Ideal for couples looking to unwind after a sports-filled holiday.

Komandoo Maldives Island Resort

📍 Lhaviyani Atoll

An intimate resort with just 65 villas, Komandoo offers a calm, romantic setting with diving, spa treatments, and private dining on the beach.

Anantara Veli Maldives Resort

📍 South Male Atoll

Overwater bungalows, a luxury spa, and fine dining make Anantara Veli perfect for a post-event getaway. The resort also offers private boat trips and snorkelling tours.

Sport Air can create combined packages where you enjoy your chosen sporting event first, then fly directly to the Maldives for a week of pure relaxation at an adults-only retreat.
