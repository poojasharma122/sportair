1. Purpose of This Policy

This policy outlines how we collect, use, store, share, and protect personal data in connection with our sports travel services. It applies to all customers, suppliers, and website users.

2. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under one or more of the following lawful bases:

  • Contractual necessity: To fulfil our agreement with you and provide the services you have booked.
  • Consent: When you have given clear consent for us to process your data for a specific purpose (e.g., marketing).
  • Legal obligation: When processing is required to comply with applicable laws.
  • Legitimate interests: For purposes that are necessary for our business, provided these interests do not override your rights and freedoms.

3. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Full name, address, email, phone number
  • Date of birth and nationality (for travel requirements)
  • Payment details (processed via secure third-party providers)
  • Travel preferences, event choices, and booking details
  • Passport and visa information where required
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and cookies

4. How We Use Personal Data

We use personal data to:

  • Process bookings and deliver travel packages
  • Communicate updates or changes to your booking
  • Issue travel documents and confirmations
  • Provide customer service and respond to inquiries
  • Send marketing updates (only where consent is given)
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

5. Data Retention

We keep your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including for legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Retention periods vary depending on the nature of the data but will be minimised wherever possible.

6. Data Sharing and Transfers

We may share your personal data with:

  • Event organisers and ticket agencies
  • Hotels, airlines, and transport providers
  • Payment processors
  • IT, website hosting, and marketing service providers

Where your data is transferred outside your country of residence, we ensure adequate protection measures are in place, such as contractual clauses or compliance with recognised international data protection frameworks.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restrict or object to processing of your data
  • Request transfer of your personal data to another provider (data portability)
  • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time

Requests should be made in writing to info@Sport-air.com. and we will respond within the legally required timeframe.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include encryption, secure storage, and restricted access to authorised staff only.

9. Marketing Preferences

Where you have opted in, we may use your contact details to send information about our packages, offers, and events. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link provided in marketing emails or by contacting us directly.

10. Complaints

If you believe we have mishandled your personal data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
